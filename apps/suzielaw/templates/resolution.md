---
id: resolution
title: Resolution (Written Consent)
description: Layout for a written resolution / unanimous written consent in lieu of a meeting — caption, recital paragraphs (WHEREAS), operative paragraphs (RESOLVED), omnibus authorization, signature block.
document_type: resolution
when_to_use: Documenting an action of a board of directors, board committee, or stockholders taken by written consent rather than at a meeting. The same structure (recitals + resolutions) is also used for individual resolutions adopted at a meeting and recorded in minutes; in that case, drop the consent caption and signature block and embed the resolutions in the minutes.
---

# Action by Written Consent of the [Board of Directors / Sole Director / Stockholders] of [COMPANY LEGAL NAME]

The undersigned, being [all of the directors / the sole director / the holders of [a majority / [percentage]] of the outstanding [voting stock / membership interests] of] **[COMPANY LEGAL NAME]**, a [STATE] [corporation / limited liability company] (the "**Company**"), acting by written consent in lieu of a meeting pursuant to [Section [X] of the [DGCL / applicable statute] and the Company's [bylaws / operating agreement]], hereby [adopt / consent to] the following resolutions, effective as of [EFFECTIVE DATE]:

## Recitals

### Background

**WHEREAS,** [recite the factual context — what is being approved, why, and any relevant background];

**WHEREAS,** [recite any prior board or stockholder approvals required as a predicate, or any agreements being entered into];

### Documents Reviewed

**WHEREAS,** the [Board of Directors / Stockholders] [has reviewed / has been presented with] [the documents to be approved], copies of which are attached hereto as **Exhibit A** (the "**Transaction Documents**");

### Best Interests Determination

**WHEREAS,** the [Board of Directors / Stockholders] has determined that [the proposed action] is in the best interests of the Company and its [stockholders / members];

## Resolutions

### Principal Authorization

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** that [the principal action — e.g. "the Company is hereby authorized to enter into the Transaction Documents on substantially the terms presented"];

### Document Approval

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that [secondary action — e.g. "the form, terms, and provisions of each Transaction Document are hereby approved"];

### Officer Authorization

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that [the officers of the Company / [SPECIFIC OFFICER]] are, and each of them hereby is, authorized and empowered, in the name and on behalf of the Company, to [execute and deliver / negotiate, execute, and deliver] the Transaction Documents, with such changes therein as the officer(s) executing the same may approve, such approval to be conclusively evidenced by such execution;

### Additional Resolutions

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that [any additional substantive resolutions — e.g. authorization to file with regulators, adopt forms, issue securities, designate officers, etc.];

## Omnibus Authorization

### General Authority

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that the officers of the Company are, and each of them hereby is, authorized and empowered, in the name and on behalf of the Company, to take or cause to be taken any and all such further actions, to execute and deliver any and all such further agreements, certificates, instruments, and documents, and to pay all such fees and expenses, as such officer(s) may deem necessary or appropriate to carry out the intent and accomplish the purposes of the foregoing resolutions, the taking of any such action or the execution and delivery of any such document to be conclusive evidence of the authorization hereunder;

### Ratification

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that any actions taken by the officers of the Company prior to the date of these resolutions that are within the authority conferred hereby are hereby ratified, confirmed, and approved in all respects;

### Counterparts

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that this consent may be executed in counterparts, including by electronic signature, each of which shall be deemed an original, and all of which together shall constitute one and the same instrument.
