id: memorandum
title: Memorandum
description: Layout for an internal legal memorandum — TO/FROM/DATE/RE header, Issue / Brief Answer / Facts / Discussion (IRAC) / Conclusion sections.
document_type: memorandum
when_to_use: Objective analysis of a legal question for a colleague or client. Tone is neutral and analytical (vs. the persuasive tone of a brief).
---

# Memorandum

**TO:** [RECIPIENT NAME, TITLE]
**FROM:** [AUTHOR NAME, TITLE]
**DATE:** [DATE]
**RE:** [SHORT DESCRIPTION OF SUBJECT MATTER]
**PRIVILEGE:** Attorney–Client Privileged · Attorney Work Product

---

## I. Issue Presented

[State the precise legal question(s) presented, in one or two sentences each. If multiple sub-issues, number them.]

1. [First issue.]
2. [Second issue.]

## II. Brief Answer

[State the conclusion in one to three sentences per issue. Begin with "Probably yes," "Probably no," or "It depends," then summarize the controlling reasoning. Do not introduce new analysis here.]

1. **Issue 1.** [Brief answer.]
2. **Issue 2.** [Brief answer.]

## III. Facts

[State the facts relevant to the analysis, in narrative form. Include only facts material to the issues. Distinguish between (a) facts established by the record or client representation and (b) assumed facts. If facts are still developing, note what has not yet been confirmed.]

## IV. Discussion

[For each issue, follow IRAC: restate the issue, lay out the governing rule (statutory text, case law, regulation), apply the rule to the facts, and conclude. Use sub-headings for each issue.]

### A. [Issue 1 Heading]

**Rule.** [State the controlling rule. Cite primary authority — statute, regulation, controlling case — with proper Bluebook citation.]

**Application.** [Apply the rule to the facts. Address counter-arguments. Discuss any factual ambiguities and how they affect the analysis. Distinguish or analogize controlling cases.]

**Conclusion.** [State the conclusion as to this sub-issue.]

### B. [Issue 2 Heading]

**Rule.** [...]

**Application.** [...]

**Conclusion.** [...]

## V. Conclusion

[Restate the overall conclusion. If recommendations or next steps are appropriate, list them here. Note any open questions or follow-up research that would strengthen the analysis.]

---

*Prepared by [AUTHOR NAME]. This memorandum reflects the law as of [DATE] and is based on the facts described above. Material changes to the facts or the governing law may alter the conclusions.*
