id: engagement-letter
title: Engagement Letter
description: Layout for a law firm's engagement letter — addressee, scope of representation, fee structure, conflicts and waiver, file retention and termination, signature block. Frames the attorney–client relationship.
document_type: engagement-letter
when_to_use: Opening a new client matter or engaging on a new scope for an existing client. Distinct from an agreement: this is a one-way letter the firm sends to the client describing the relationship, requiring the client's countersignature.
---

# [LAW FIRM LETTERHEAD]

[DATE]

**[CLIENT CONTACT NAME]**
[CLIENT TITLE / ENTITY]
[CLIENT ADDRESS]

Re: **Engagement of [LAW FIRM NAME] — [MATTER DESCRIPTION]**

Dear [CLIENT CONTACT]:

Thank you for selecting [LAW FIRM NAME] (the "**Firm**", "we", or "us") to represent [CLIENT LEGAL NAME] (the "**Client**", "you", or "your") in connection with [MATTER DESCRIPTION]. This letter sets forth the terms of our engagement.

## 1. Scope of Representation

We will represent you in connection with the following: [describe the scope precisely — e.g. "negotiating and documenting your acquisition of [TARGET], including drafting transaction documents, advising on regulatory matters, and assisting at closing"]. Our representation does not include any matter not specifically described above. If additional matters arise that you wish us to handle, we will agree on a separate scope and confirm it in writing.

## 2. Identity of Client

The Client in this matter is [CLIENT LEGAL NAME] only. We do not represent any of its [affiliates, parents, subsidiaries, directors, officers, employees, or shareholders] in this matter unless we expressly agree otherwise in writing.

## 3. Staffing

[NAME], [TITLE], will have primary responsibility for this matter. [Other lawyers and staff who will work on the matter — typically partners, associates, paralegals.] We may staff the matter as appropriate to handle it efficiently.

## 4. Fees

### 4.1 Hourly Rates
Our fees for this matter will be calculated based on the time spent by lawyers and other timekeepers, at our standard hourly rates in effect from time to time. Current rates for the timekeepers expected to work on this matter are:

- [NAME], [TITLE]: $[RATE]/hour
- [NAME], [TITLE]: $[RATE]/hour

Rates are reviewed annually, typically effective [JANUARY 1] each year. We will notify you of rate changes.

### 4.2 [Alternative — Fixed Fee / Capped Fee / Contingent]
[Use if applicable.]

### 4.3 Expenses
In addition to fees, we will charge for actual out-of-pocket expenses incurred on your behalf, including filing fees, court costs, expert fees, courier and overnight delivery, and similar items. We do not separately charge for routine in-house items such as photocopying, local telephone, and email.

### 4.4 Estimates
Any fee estimate is a good-faith estimate based on assumptions current at the time and is not a cap unless specifically described as such in writing.

## 5. Billing

We will issue invoices [monthly]. Invoices are due within [thirty (30)] days of receipt. Late amounts may bear interest at [the lesser of 1% per month or the maximum rate permitted by law]. Please review invoices promptly and contact us with any questions; we are happy to discuss any item.

## 6. Retainer [if applicable]

We request an initial retainer of $[AMOUNT], to be applied against fees and expenses. We may request the retainer to be replenished as the matter progresses.

## 7. Conflicts of Interest

### 7.1 Conflicts Check
We have conducted a conflicts check and are not aware of any conflict that would prevent us from representing you in this matter.

### 7.2 Advance Waiver
The Firm represents many clients across a wide range of matters and industries. By signing this engagement letter, you consent to the Firm's representation of other current and future clients in matters unrelated to your representation, even if those clients are competitors of yours, provided that (a) the matter is not substantially related to our representation of you, (b) we do not use your confidential information against you, and (c) [the matter does not involve direct adversity to you in litigation, except with your separate consent].

## 8. Confidentiality

We will treat your information as confidential in accordance with the [State] Rules of Professional Conduct. We may include your name and a general description of the matter on our list of representative engagements unless you instruct us otherwise.

## 9. File Retention

At the conclusion of the matter, we will retain your file for [seven (7)] years, after which we may destroy it unless you have requested in writing that we return it to you.

## 10. Termination

You may terminate our representation at any time by written notice. We may withdraw subject to the [State] Rules of Professional Conduct. Termination does not affect your obligation to pay for services rendered and expenses incurred through the date of termination.

## 11. No Guarantee of Outcome

We will use our best professional judgment, but we cannot and do not guarantee any particular outcome.

## 12. Governing Law and Disputes

This engagement is governed by the laws of [STATE]. Any dispute arising under it will be resolved by [arbitration / the courts located in [JURISDICTION]].

## 13. Entire Agreement

This letter, together with any addenda referenced herein, constitutes our entire engagement agreement. Any amendment must be in writing and signed by both parties.
